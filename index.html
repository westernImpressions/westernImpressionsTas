About the Studio

Nestled in Zeehan on Tasmania’s wild West Coast, Western Impressions is a lino printmaking studio where each piece is a reflection of place: stormy harbours, Huon pine driftwood, Maugean skates, and the delicate balance between nature and industry.

The Story Behind “Coexistence”

Salmon farms and endangered skates may seem at odds — but Jaeseok’s first print explores how balance is possible. With Korean philosophy and West Coast symbolism, Coexistence is a visual statement in favour of harmony.
